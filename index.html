Why Super Shiba?
Pure security of trust
We believe that honesty with holders is essential for growth so we our team will make everything as transparent as possible to ensure our investors that everything is clear. We have renounced ownership of the contract and locked the funds in the liquidity pool, the token is rug-proof because it has no owner. It will exist forever on BSC, unaltered. So rest easy, knowing that funds are very very safu.
Charity donation
3% of tax goes charity donation to homeless dogs. We're working with our partner DogsTrust (https://www.dogstrust.org.uk/donation/). Your kind donations help to provide life changing care and forever homes for over 14,000 of our furry friends. We really appreciate your support and dogs do appreciate too.
Smart deflationary mechanism to prevent price dump
In the first 7 days after the launch, there will be an increased commission for the sale in order to avoid a price drop due to bots. Also we're working on the new dump prevention system. Moreover in the first 30 days 1%of tokens will be burned to pump token's price.
